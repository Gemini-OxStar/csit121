Thank you for visiting the website of Bean and Bloom. Bean & Bloom Café is a neighborhood coffeehouse known for its in-house bean roasting, pastries, and calm atmosphere. The café is a popular spot for reading, studying, and meeting friends.
Bean & Bloom emphasizes ethically sourced beans, seasonal specialty drinks, and house-made baked goods. Sustainability is at the heart of the business, which offers rewards for reusable cups, uses compostable materials, and partners with local growers and artists.

How We Do Business

We have 6 blends to choose from depending on the current season. our baked goods also change with the seasons. With this, customers will want to visit more often to try each blend that we provide each season, and you'll seldom get the same thing over and over again. Flexibility is key in life, as we say.
