Labs 1 & 2

Published on September 28, 2025 by Timothy Kluver

Working on these assignments was daunting at first, but I started to get the gist of it as I continued working on the first two labs. It helps that I have experience from taking this class in the previous semester.

Three Key Factors I Have Learned:

  1. The difference between head, header, and heading tags.
    The head tag is used to capture all of the information needed to make the webpage run. Such info includes stylesheet links, and other general document information.
    The header tag is a custom tag you place in the body of the html. You ideally want to use this tag to store navigational aids, headings, logos, etc.
    Headings are keyword-infused titles that help the search engines understand the structure of your page content.
  2. The parts of a URL. The scheme (HTTP, HTTPS,) domain name (www.something.com,) the path to the file (/this/that/theother,) parameters (?key1=value1&key2=value2,) and an anchor (#somewhereinthedocument.)
  3. To make a sentence in a paragraph tag italicized, you must insert 'i' tags around the words you want this effect on.

  • The Difference Between Head, Header, and Headings
